### FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

-   **Is this really free?**
    -   Yes. The audit tool is completely free to use. We may offer paid consultations for teams that want a more in-depth analysis, but the core tool is free.

-   **Do I need to connect my billing accounts?**
    -   No. The audit is based on the information you provide. This keeps the process fast and secure. You don't need to grant any permissions or sign in.

-   **How does the audit work?**
    -   Our engine compares your spending and seat count against the official pricing plans of popular AI tools. It identifies common inefficiencies, such as being on the wrong plan for your team size or missing out on volume discounts.

-   **Is my data safe?**
    -   Yes. The audit data you provide is used to generate a public, anonymized report. If you choose to enter your email, it is stored securely and is never shared.
